Delete Your Account & Data

SwipeTrainer: Stock Charts — developed by Tomer Aminizar

This page explains how to delete your SwipeTrainer account and the data associated with it. SwipeTrainer can be used anonymously (no account required); signing in with Google or Apple is optional and only used to sync your stats across devices.

Option 1 — Clear your data in the app

To erase your play history (swipes, scores, accuracy, streak) at any time:

  • Open SwipeTrainer
  • Go to Settings (the gear icon on the Home screen)
  • Tap Reset Stats and confirm

This permanently deletes all of your attempt history from our servers.

Option 2 — Delete your entire account & all data

To request full deletion of your account and every piece of data associated with it, email us from the address you signed in with:

📧 swipetrainer.app@gmail.com with the subject line Delete my account.

We will permanently delete your account and all associated data within 30 days of the request and confirm by email.

What data is deleted

The following are permanently removed:

  • Your profile (username, display name)
  • Your sign-in identity (email / name from Google or Apple)
  • All attempt history, scores, streaks, and daily-challenge results

No data is retained after deletion, except where required by law. We do not sell your data, and we keep no backups beyond 30 days.
